Return & Refund Policy

30-Day Return Window

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange.

Return Requirements

To be eligible for a return, the item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging with all tags intact
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)
How to Return
  1. Initiate Return: Go to My Orders and select the item
  2. Select Reason: Choose why you want to return
  3. Schedule Pickup: We'll arrange free pickup from your address
  4. Get Refund: Refund processed within 7-10 business days
Refund Method
  • Original Payment Method: Refund to the same method used for purchase
  • Store Credit: Optional instant store credit for faster processing
  • Processing Time: 7-10 business days after we receive the item
Non-Returnable Items
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Hygiene products (opened)
  • Sale items (final sale)
  • Gift cards
Return Shipping

We provide FREE return pickup for all eligible returns. Our courier will collect the package from your doorstep at no additional cost.
